View and Print Previous Time Records

  1. Log in to Employee Self Service (ESS).

  2. On the top menu bar, click Time & Attendance.

  3. On the left Time and Attendance Menu, click Attendance History.

                                      Click the image below to see additional screen and field details.

  4. Review the information on the right and note that:

    • At the top, the View attendance records for year drop-down automatically defaults to the current year.

    • In the middle, your Active Attendance Records appear. Click on a current or outstanding time record here to open, edit, and submit it.

    • On the bottom, your Submitted Attendance Records appear. These are time records that have already been submitted for the selected year.

  5. If necessary, on the top right, click View attendance records for the year drop-down arrow and select the year for which you want to view submitted time.

  6. Under Submitted Attendance Records, click the row with the desired time record date range.

  7. When the Attendance record for... window appears, complete one of the following:

    • To print the record, click Print Record in the bottom right corner.
      When the Time and Attendance Record PDF appears, right-click on the PDF, select Print, complete the print dialog box, and click Print.
      Close the window.

    • To close the window, click Exit in the bottom right corner.
