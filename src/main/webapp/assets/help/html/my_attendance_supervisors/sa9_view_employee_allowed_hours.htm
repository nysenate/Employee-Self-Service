View Employee Allowed Hours

(T&A Supervisors Only)

  1. Log in to Employee Self Service (ESS).

  2. On the top menu bar, click Time & Attendance.

  3. On the left Time and Attendance Menu, under Manage Employees, click Employee Allowed Hours.

                                      Click the image below to see additional screen and field details.

  4. On the right, click the View Allowed Hours for Employee drop-down arrow and select the name of the employee whose hours you want to review.
